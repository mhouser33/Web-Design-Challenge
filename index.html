Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temp

In this project we are looking to display and better answer the question, "What's the weather like as we approach the equator?" We first created a Python script to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. We did this by pulling data from the OpenWeatherMap API.

Once the dataset was created, we built a series of scatterplots using Matplotlub to showcase realtionships between:

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. latitude

After creating these scatterplots, we ran linear regressions on each relationship byseparating them into Northern Hemisphere and Southern Hemisphere:

  • Northern Hemisphere - Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Northern Hemisphere - Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Northern Hemisphere - Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Northern Hemisphere - Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
  • Southern Hemisphere - Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
  • Southern Hemisphere - Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Southern Hemisphere - Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Southern Hemisphere - Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude

Within this site, you will be able to view the datasets from which we gathered our analysis and visualizations. It will also include correlations found and different interpretations of our findings.
